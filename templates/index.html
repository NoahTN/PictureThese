About

Built by a team of Claudio Vertemara, Kevin Piffero, Jesus Andres Bernal Lopez, and Noah Tolentino Nguyen as a more fun and interactive way to learn how things are said in other languages.

Using the Google Cloud Vision API, we can analyze images and specify their contents, then translate them into a language of your choosing. Hit the “Upload File” button and we'll take it from there.
